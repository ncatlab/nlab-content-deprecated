## Definition

A **$\Pi$-pretopos** is a [[pretopos]] that is also a [[locally cartesian closed category]].


## Related concepts

* A [[Î W-pretopos]] is a $\Pi$-pretopos that also has all [[W-types]].

* A [[predicative topos]] is $\Pi W$-pretopos with [[axiom of multiple choice]].
